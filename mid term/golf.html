South Kent
Philosophy

Playing hard and playing together is at the heart
of South Kent School’s athletic program. Our
coaches emphasize integrity, intelligence, and i
ntensity in team play. We believe in building strong,
unified teams—win or lose.

Our goal is to use athletics as a tool to prepare
young men for the challenges they will face throughout
their lives. Whether a student-athlete is competing at
the collegiate level or exploring a new sport for the
first time, there is a place for him in South Kent athletics.

Every aspect of our program is designed to ensure that students
receive high-quality instruction and access to state-of-the-art
facilities. Practices, one-on-one coaching, and strength
training are all tailored to foster excellence—both on game
day and in life.

South Kent athletes are challenged daily and supported
in their drive to improve. Our mission is to nurture
the entire student body and to develop student-athletes
of excellence.

NURTURING
EXCELLENCE

Playing hard and playing together is at the heart
of South Kent School’s athletic program. Our
coaches emphasize integrity, intelligence, and i
ntensity in team play. We believe in building strong,
unified teams—win or lose.

South Kent School offers a top-tier private school
education while at the same time providing our
students with the opportunity to play the most
competitive AAA ice hockey in the Northeast.
South Kent School Hockey prides itself on a
history of developing self-discipline, commitment
, sportsmanship, and a passion for the game at multiple
levels. Players learn not only the critical skills of the
game, but lessons in leadership, commitment and lifestyle
management that will last a lifetime. Our dedicated coaching
staff have in-depth experience that inspires each student
athlete to achieve their personal athletic goals. Through
daily training, competitive matches, and an overall
commitment to excellence, South Kent Hockey has a proven
track record of success. In recent years, South Kent School
has claimed many New England and tournament Championships,
and made multiple appearances in the USA Hockey National
Championships. In 2018 and 2019, SKS Hockey was named National
Champions.

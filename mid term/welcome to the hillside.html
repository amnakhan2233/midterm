Welcome to South Kent School

Since its founding in 1923, South Kent School has been a school that understands the way boys learn. Our faculty and staff are committed to providing the highest quality student experience that builds a deeper connection to learning.

Students discover more about themselves when the application of knowledge is required beyond the classroom and carried into real-world experiences. We help students develop both the passion and skill required to pursue their interests.

As a result, our students can discover and take advantage of transformative opportunities that they may have never realized otherwise.

Jason Lefevre Director of Admissions

We look forward to seeing you soon on the Hillside or on the road.

Get to Know South Kent School


South Kent is a school for boys and focuses with an understanding on the way boys learn. Our curriculum is tailored to
the individual and provides a wide range of opportunities for them to expand their interests and hone their focus.
South Kent School prepares young men to succeed in college and thrive as thoughtful and engaged citizens.

100 Years of Excellence

With a rich legacy spanning a century, South Kent School stands as a beacon of excellence in educating young men for
over 100 years. Our institution’s longstanding commitment to understanding and catering to the unique learning styles of
boys has endowed us with invaluable insights into their educational needs. Recognizing the significance of our history, we
embrace it as the foundation upon which we innovate for the future. Drawing upon this wealth of experience, we continue
to evolve, ensuring that each generation of students benefits from the wisdom of the past while embracing the
opportunities of tomorrow. Explore our Trinity of Values and connect with our Admissions Team.
